Fencing Practice Video

This video shows a short fencing bout recorded during a practice session of the Fencing Club at Muhlenberg College. It highlights basic épée footwork, timing, and referee calls in a collegiate club environment.



About the Bout

  • Location: The Field House in Muhlenberg College's Life Sports Center
  • Participants: Aaron Schlossberg vs. Benjamin Dicker
  • Referee: Coach Paul McAndrew
  • Filmed by: Coach Noah Katz



Why This Video Matters

This clip demonstrates beginner-to-intermediate fencing techniques in a low-pressure club setting, intended for students interested in learning fencing with or without prior experience.
